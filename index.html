Simple rules

Tap a direction and the current shape folds into new tiles. The original tiles stay in place, so each move doubles the covered area.

Readable boards

Soft block textures, clear target cells, and lightweight animations keep the puzzle state easy to scan on iPhone and iPad.

Private by design

Progress and audio settings stay on your device. The game does not need internet access, analytics, ads, or sign-in.
