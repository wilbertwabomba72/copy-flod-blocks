copy flod blocks Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 17, 2026

copy flod blocks is designed as a fully offline single-player puzzle game.

Data Collection

copy flod blocks does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal data. The app does not require an account, email address, phone number, location permission, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, advertising identifier, or any other personal information.

Local Game Data

The app stores gameplay progress and preferences locally on your device. This may include unlocked levels, completed levels, best move counts, last played level, music preference, and sound effects preference. This data stays on your device and is not sent to us or to any third party.

Network Use

copy flod blocks does not require an internet connection for gameplay. The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, social login, cloud sync, push notifications, or third-party tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

copy flod blocks is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

You can delete local game data by deleting the app from your device. Reinstalling the app may start a new local save.

Changes to This Policy

If the app changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated before the change is released.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact the developer through the App Store product page.
